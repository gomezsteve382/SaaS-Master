MASTER TRANSPONDER BLOCK
========================
Location:        0x0226 (mirror copy at 0x0232, separated by FF FF)
16-byte secret:  59 02 2F 55 42 BE 76 44 EC 20 28 C5 3A D4 D6 54
18-byte w/trailer: 59 02 2F 55 42 BE 76 44 EC 20 28 C5 3A D4 D6 54 2E 00   (trailer 2E 00)
Preceding bytes @0x0224: FF FF
ASCII of low region: (none printable - binary secret)

This is the per-vehicle master used by the RFHUB. The index byte is believed to be
derived from KeyID under THIS secret (keyed MAC) OR assigned by the RFHUB at the
0x0401 key-learn. Brute force of simple ops (xor/add/sub/CRC8 poly 07,1D,31) vs
KeyID and vs master bytes produced ZERO matches.
