**1. Modules must be physically installed and flashed first**
- BCM and RFHUB need to be written and installed in the vehicle

**2. Confirm PCM SEC6**
- You'll want to verify `08 A1 C5 E7 BA 30` is in the GPEC2A

**3. Key programming method**
- Use a **J2534 pass-thru** with wiTECH 2.0 or AutoAuth-licensed tool
